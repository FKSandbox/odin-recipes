Corn-BaconGuacamole

Description

This is a variation of Guacamole Recipe with a twist(Bacon+Corn) some of it ingredients are ofcourse avocados, corn, bacon,tomato, cilantro, lime juice and jalapenos. it is Delish!

Ingredients

  • 2 ears of Corn
  • 3 avocados
  • 1/2 onion
  • Cilantro
  • 6 cooked Bacon slices
  • 1/3 cup of chopped pickled
  • salt and lime juice

Directions

  1. Grill 2 ears of corn until charred; cut off the kernels
  2. Mash 3 avocados
  3. stir in 1/4 cup diced white onion, 1/2 cup each diced plum tomato and chopped cilantro
    4. >
  4. Add the corn, 6 slices crumbled cooked bacon and 1/3 cup chopped pickled jalapenos.
  5. Add lime juice and salt to taste.
