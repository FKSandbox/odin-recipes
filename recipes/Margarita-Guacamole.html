Margarita Guacamole

Description

What goes together better than margaritas and guacamole? Nothing, thatâ€™s what! This guacamole gets a zing from lots of lime and orange juice and a real kick from a big splash of tequila.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados
  • 1/4 cup diced scallion
  • 1/2 cup each diced plum tomato
  • Chopped cilantro
  • 1 diced seeded jalapeno
  • 2 limes
  • juice of 1/2 orange
  • 3 tablespoons tequila

Directions

  1. Mash 3 avocados
  2. stir in 1/4 cup diced scallion
  3. Add 1/2 cup each diced plum tomato and chopped cilantro
  4. Add 1 diced seeded jalapeno,
  5. Add zest and juice of 1 lime and juice of 1/2 orange
  6. Add 3 tablespoons tequila.
  7. Mix well all ingridients together
  8. Serve in a salt-rimmed bowl with lime wedges
